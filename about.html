Our Mission.

Our mission is to create a platform that simplifies the experience of traveling by promoting safety through communication and awareness, creating a sense of global community in a safe and sustainable way, and breaking down cultural divides.

The Birth of Rootless

Rootless is a passion project that was born out of a love for exploration, travel and a bewilderment of the world. While traveling and exploring there are so many unknowns. Rootless creates a space to optimize your time and experience when planning your travels. When comparing and discussing our personal travel experiences with others, the need became clear for an outlet to access more opportunity in one place. Rootless was born, a platform for a true global travel community.

Who Do We Serve

Travel has long been viewed as a luxury. A trip is often seen as a privilege, only accessible for the more fortunate. Our goal at Rootless, is to make sure travel is available and open to all. We are breaking down barriers that have long been built up by society, using education as our tool. Rootless aims to inform everyone about the opportunities, adventures and possibilities in store. We hope we can help you learn more about how accessible many wonders of the world can be.
